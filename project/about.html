What is Software Engineering

Software engineering is the systematic application of engineering principles to design, develop, test, and maintain reliable software systems. It combines creativity, expertise, and effective management to solve complex real-world problems.

Importance of Software Engineering

Software engineering is essential for designing, developing, testing, and maintaining reliable and scalable systems that power technology. It ensures quality, efficiency, and innovation across industries, transforming ideas into robust solutions.
